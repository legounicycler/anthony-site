Who am I?

I am a self-published author, extreme unicyclist, YouTube creator, and lover of all weird hobbies.

I am currently a senior at Stockdale High School in Bakerfield, California. I am planning to attend a 4 year university to study aerospace engineering.

My book.

I wrote and self-published this really cool book with my friend. It has magic, time travel, wizards, dragons, dwarves, and lots of sarcasm. Inspired by the Percy Jackson series, this book is aimed at elementary and junior high school kids who love a witty and humorous adventure novel.

The entire novel was written in 6 months during my seventh grade year. You can purchase it on Amazon for $12.99 or visit the website (designed by yours truly) at www.sonoftimechronicles.com.

Unicycling.

I am a member of something called The UniCrew. We are a group of extreme unicyclists co-founded by myself and Jason Colaw. We make videos, ride in local performances, and even compete internationally.

To find out more information about what exactly we do, visit theunicrew.com (website also designed by yours truly).
