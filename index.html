A what??

When you think of unicycling, you probably think of a juggling act in a circus. You probably don't think about an extreme sport that includes riding black diamond mountain biking trails, grinding handrails, and traveling the world.

That's the type of unicyling I do. (And yes, I can also juggle.) I started unicycling in highschool with a group called The Unicew, and now I'm an internationally ranked competetive unicyclist who mostly rides in the mountains nowadays. I also did a cross country unipacking trip for 300+ miles a few years back.
