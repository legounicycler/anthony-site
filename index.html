UNICYCLISt

A what??

When you think of unicycling, you probably think of a juggling act in a circus. You probably don't think about an extreme sport that includes riding black diamond mountain biking trails, grinding handrails, and traveling the world.

That's the type of unicyling I do. (And yes, I can also juggle.) I started unicycling in highschool with a group called The Unicew, and now I'm an internationally ranked competetive unicyclist who nowadays mostly rides in the mountains. I also did a cross country uni-packing trip for 300+ miles a few years back.

If you want to see what that looks like, you can check out The Unicrew YouTube channel or my personal Instagram. You can also watch some of my favorite unicycling highlight videos right here.

Son of Time Chronicles

Son of Time Chronicles is a time-travel fantasy book series that I co-authored with my close friend and fellow adventurer Tyler Ferris. We have been writing together since middle school!

What started as a handful of ideas has now developed into a trilogy of thrilling adventure books for children and young adults. The first book, Son of Time was published in 2017. The second, The Sierra Rod, was published in 2019. And the third, Age of Masters is currently being written!

If you'd like to read Son of Time or The Sierra Rod, you can visit our website. You can also visit our website to purchase the books or other merchandise such as stickers and t-shirts. You can also following our Instagram and Facebook pages to stay up-to-date with our progress on Age of Masters and learn about our other adventures.
